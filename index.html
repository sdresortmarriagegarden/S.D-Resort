Wedding Hall FAQs

Quick answers for families searching for a wedding hall, marriage garden, or reception venue in Sikar.

Where is S.D. Resort & Marriage Garden located?

S.D. Resort & Marriage Garden is located at NH-52, Dhod Rd, Sikar, Rajasthan 332001.

How many guests can the venue host?

Our wedding venue can support large celebrations with guest capacity up to 5000+ people, depending on the event layout and function requirements.

Is the venue suitable for weddings and receptions?

Yes. The marriage garden is suitable for weddings, receptions, engagements, sangeet, mehendi, haldi ceremonies, anniversaries, and family functions.

Are rooms available for wedding guests?

Yes. AC and Non-AC rooms are available for families and guests who need a comfortable space during wedding functions.

Do you provide catering and decoration support?

Our team supports wedding arrangements including catering coordination, stage decor, lighting, guest seating, music setup, and event flow.

How can I book the marriage garden?

You can call +91 96539 87886 or +91 99837 39485, email sdresortmarriagegarden@gmail.com, send a WhatsApp message, or submit the enquiry form.
